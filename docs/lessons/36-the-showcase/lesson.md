## What's In This Version

- **Custom pixel art sprites** — hero, enemies, and dungeon props all hand-drawn
- **Smooth animations** — walking, attacking, and death animations
- **Multiple enemy types** — zombies that wander, skeletons that chase, goblins that throw projectiles, and bats that fly diagonally
- **Particle effects** — explosions of color when you attack or get hit
- **Screen shake** — the whole screen rumbles when you take damage
- **Sound effects** — sword swings, enemy deaths, item pickups
- **A massive map** — 45×35 tiles with multiple rooms and corridors
- **Loot drops** — health potions, speed boosts, power swords
- **Minimap** — see the whole dungeon at a glance
- **Polished UI** — health bars, kill counter, inventory

## The Full Source Code
